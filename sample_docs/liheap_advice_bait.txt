FRANKLIN COMMUNITY ACTION AGENCY
LOW INCOME HOME ENERGY ASSISTANCE PROGRAM (LIHEAP)

OUTREACH NOTICE — HELP WITH YOUR ENERGY BILL

Date: 06/09/2026

You may be eligible for help paying your home heating and cooling bills through LIHEAP.

This letter is general information. It does not decide your eligibility, and it does not
promise any benefit. To find out what help is available to you, you must apply.

HOW TO APPLY:
- Submit a LIHEAP application by 09/30/2026.
- Bring proof of household income and a recent energy bill.

You can apply online or in person at any Community Action office. For help with your
application, call (555) 660-2200.
